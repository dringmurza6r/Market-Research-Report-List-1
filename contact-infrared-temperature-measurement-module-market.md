<p><p>The Contact Infrared Temperature Measurement Module market is categorized into Digital Signal and Analog Signal types. In contrast, Analog Signal modules produce continuous voltage or current signals that reflect temperature changes. These are often preferred in simpler applications where real-time monitoring is essential, though they may lack the accuracy and processing capabilities of digital systems.</p></p>
