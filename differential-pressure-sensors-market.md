The market is primarily divided into two types: digital and analog sensors. Digital differential pressure sensors provide precise readings and can interface with digital systems for advanced monitoring and control. Both types cater to diverse industries, including HVAC, manufacturing, and process control, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
