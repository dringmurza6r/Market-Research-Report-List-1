<p><p>A liquid ring pump is a type of positive displacement pump that utilizes a rotating impeller to create a ring of liquid, typically water, which forms a seal between the impeller and the casing. This configuration allows for efficient handling of gases, vapors, and, to some extent, liquids. Liquid ring pumps are commonly used in applications requiring vacuum creation, such as in food processing, chemical manufacturing, and various industrial processes.</p><p>The Liquid Ring Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period.
