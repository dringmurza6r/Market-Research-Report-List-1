<p><ul><li>Post Industrial Glass Fiber</li><li>Recycled GFRP Glass Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Recycled Glass Fiber Market is primarily categorized into two types: Post Industrial Glass Fiber and Recycled GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer). Post Industrial Glass Fiber is derived from manufacturing processes where excess or off-spec fiber is collected and repurposed, reducing waste. Recycled GFRP involves reclaiming glass fibers from the disassembly of end-of-life products, such as composites, for reuse. Both types contribute to sustainability in the composites industry, promoting the circular economy by lowering resource consumption and environmental impact.</p></p>
