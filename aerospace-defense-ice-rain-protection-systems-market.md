<p><p>The Aerospace Defense Ice Rain Protection Systems market comprises two main types: Anti-Icing Systems and De-Icing Systems. Anti-Icing Systems prevent ice formation on aircraft surfaces by employing heating elements or special coatings, ensuring safety during flight in icy conditions. In contrast, De-Icing Systems actively remove ice that has already formed, often using mechanical or thermal methods. Both systems are crucial for enhancing aircraft performance and safety in adverse weather conditions, thereby playing a vital role in aerospace operations.</p></p>
