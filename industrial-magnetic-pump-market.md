<p><strong>The Industrial Magnetic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial magnetic pump market is primarily segmented into two types: stationary shaft and rotating shaft pumps. Stationary shaft magnetic pumps feature a fixed outer casing with the magnetic drive situated outside the fluid being pumped, making them ideal for handling corrosive or hazardous liquids. In contrast, rotating shaft magnetic pumps utilize a rotating magnetic field to drive the impeller, offering enhanced efficiency and flow control. Both types minimize leakage risks, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
