<p><p>The benchtop parts washer market primarily comprises two types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based systems. Solvent-based washers utilize chemical solvents to effectively remove oils and greases, making them ideal for heavy-duty cleaning tasks. In contrast, water/aqueous-based washers employ water mixed with detergents, offering a more environmentally friendly alternative suitable for lighter cleaning applications. Each type serves distinct needs, with solvent-based options often providing faster results and higher efficacy on stubborn contaminants, while aqueous-based solutions are generally safer and easier to maintain.</p></p>
