<p><p>The all-in-one printer market can be categorized based on connectivity technologies, which include USB, Wi-Fi, and USB/Wi-Fi options. USB printers connect directly to computers, offering reliable, fast printing but limited mobility. Wi-Fi printers enable wireless connections, allowing multiple devices to print from anywhere within the network, enhancing convenience. Meanwhile, USB/Wi-Fi printers offer a dual approach, supporting both wired and wireless connections, giving users flexibility in how they choose to print based on their needs and environments.</p></p>
