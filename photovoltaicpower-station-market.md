<p><p>The photovoltaic power station market is primarily divided into two types: stand-alone and grid-connected photovoltaic systems. Stand-alone photovoltaic systems operate independently, generating electricity for remote locations without access to the grid, relying on battery storage for energy management. In contrast, grid-connected photovoltaic systems are integrated with the electrical grid, allowing excess electricity to be fed back into the grid while drawing power when needed. This integration enhances energy security and promotes the use of renewable energy sources on a larger scale.</p></p>
