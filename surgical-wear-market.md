<p><p>The surgical wear market is broadly categorized into two types: disposable wear and reusable wear. Disposable wear includes single-use items like gowns, masks, and gloves, designed to enhance hygiene and prevent cross-contamination during surgeries. In contrast, reusable wear consists of durable textiles such as surgical gowns and drapes that can be sterilized and utilized multiple times, promoting cost-effectiveness and sustainability. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining safety and efficacy in surgical environments.</p></p>
