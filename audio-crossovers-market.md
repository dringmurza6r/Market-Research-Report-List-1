<p><strong>The Audio Crossovers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio crossovers are essential components in sound systems, dividing audio signals into different frequency ranges for speakers. The active crossover market utilizes powered systems, where signals are divided before amplification, allowing for precise control and optimization of sound quality. In contrast, the passive crossover market employs passive components, such as capacitors and inductors, to divide frequencies after amplification, resulting in simpler installations but less control. Both markets serve distinct needs in audio performance, catering to different user preferences and system requirements.</p></p>
