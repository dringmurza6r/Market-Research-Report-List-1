<p><p>The treadmill belts market primarily consists of two types: PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) and TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane). Conversely, TPU belts offer enhanced flexibility, superior wear resistance, and better shock absorption, catering to high-performance and commercial treadmills.
