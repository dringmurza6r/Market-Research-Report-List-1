Dolby Digital delivers true multi-channel audio, enhancing cinematic experiences with 5.1 channels. DTS offers an alternative to Dolby, known for high-quality audio and dynamic sound environments. Each system caters to different consumer needs, from home theaters to professional audio setups, underscoring their significance in multimedia entertainment.</p></p>
