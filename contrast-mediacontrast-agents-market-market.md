<p><p>The Contrast Media/Contrast Agents market comprises various types, including iodinated agents, gadolinium-based agents, barium compounds, and microbubbles. Iodinated agents are primarily used in CT scans and X-rays to enhance imaging clarity. Gadolinium agents are essential for magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), improving soft tissue visualization. Barium is utilized in gastrointestinal imaging for better depiction of the digestive tract. Microbubbles are employed in ultrasound imaging, enhancing the contrast and visibility of blood flow and tissue perfusion.</p></p>
