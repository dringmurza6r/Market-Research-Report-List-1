<p><p>The indicating silica gel market features two primary types: blue indicating silica gel and orange indicating silica gel. Blue silica gel contains cobalt chloride, changing from blue to pink as it absorbs moisture, providing a clear visual indication of humidity levels. In contrast, orange silica gel uses an organic indicator, shifting from orange to green when it absorbs moisture. Both types are widely used in desiccant applications across industries, ensuring moisture control to protect products during storage and transport.</p></p>
