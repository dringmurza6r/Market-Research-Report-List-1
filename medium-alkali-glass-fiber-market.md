<p><p>The Medium Alkali Glass Fiber market is segmented into two main types based on molding processes: One Molding and Two Molding. One Molding involves a single-step process where the glass fibers are combined with resin and molded into the desired shape in one operation, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Two Molding utilizes a two-step process, where glass fibers are first shaped and cured, then subjected to a second molding for enhanced structural integrity and complexity in design.</p></p>
