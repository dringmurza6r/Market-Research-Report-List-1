<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) resins, commonly known as acetal, are categorized into two main types for medical instruments: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene features a high degree of crystallinity, offering excellent dimensional stability and durability, making it suitable for precision components. Co-polyoxymethylene, on the other hand, incorporates various functional groups to enhance toughness and chemical resistance, providing flexibility for diverse medical applications. Both types are essential for producing reliable, high-performance medical devices that meet stringent regulatory standards.</p></p>
