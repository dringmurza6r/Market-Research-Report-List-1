<p><p>The medical transparent film dressing market is primarily divided into two types: roll type and piece type. In contrast, piece type dressings come in pre-cut shapes and sizes, ready for immediate use on specific wound areas. Both types provide a protective barrier, promoting optimal healing while maintaining visibility for monitoring the wound without compromising sterility.</p></p>
