<p><p>The surgical electrical staplers market is categorized into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable staplers are designed for single-use, offering convenience, sterility, and reduced risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for surgeries where quick setup and disposal are crucial. Reusable staplers, on the other hand, are built for multiple uses, typically requiring sterilization between procedures. While they may have a higher upfront cost, they can be more economical over time, appealing to facilities with high surgical volumes.</p></p>
