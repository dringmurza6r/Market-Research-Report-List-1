<p><p>The household air disinfectant market is divided into two main types: chemical disinfectants and physical disinfectants. Chemical disinfectants include sprays, aerosols, and wipes that use various chemicals to kill airborne pathogens, often providing immediate results and longer-lasting effects. In contrast, physical disinfectants utilize methods such as UV light or ionizers to purify the air without the use of chemicals, focusing on disrupting microbial growth through physical processes. Both types aim to enhance indoor air quality and promote health.</p></p>
