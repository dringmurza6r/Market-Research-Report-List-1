<p><p>The semiconductor mask blanks market consists of two primary types: quartz masks and soda-lime glass masks. Quartz masks, made from high-purity quartz, offer excellent thermal stability and transparency, making them ideal for advanced lithography processes. Soda-lime glass masks, while more cost-effective, provide lower performance due to their comparatively lower thermal stability and light transmission. Both types serve essential roles in photomask production, catering to different technological needs and economic considerations in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
