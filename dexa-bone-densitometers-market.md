<p><ul><li>Axial Bone Densitometer</li><li>Peripheral Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DEXA bone densitometer market is segmented into axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers primarily measure bone density in the spine and hip, offering high precision and reliability, making them suitable for diagnosing osteoporosis. Peripheral bone densitometers, on the other hand, measure bone density in peripheral areas like the wrist or heel. They are portable, cost-effective, and ideal for screening purposes, often used in primary care settings to assess fracture risk in patients efficiently.</p></p>
