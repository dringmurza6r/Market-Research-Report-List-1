<p><p>The Blood Purification Device Market encompasses several types, including Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and other methods. CBP is designed for critically ill patients, facilitating continuous filtration of blood to remove toxins and manage fluid balance. Hemodialysis, on the other hand, involves periodic sessions for patients with kidney failure, where blood is filtered externally through a dialyzer. Other market types may include peritoneal dialysis and various innovative devices aimed at improving efficiency and patient comfort in blood purification processes.</p></p>
