<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Oral Antidiabetic Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfonylureas</li><li>Meglitinides</li><li>Biguanides</li><li>Thiazolidinediones</li><li>Alpha-glucosidase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral antidiabetic agents market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Sulfonylureas** stimulate insulin secretion from the pancreas.</p><p>2. **Meglitinides** also promote insulin release but act more rapidly and for shorter durations.</p><p>3. **Biguanides**, primarily metformin, enhance insulin sensitivity and reduce glucose production in the liver.</p><p>4. **Thiazolidinediones** improve insulin sensitivity in muscle and fat tissues.</p><p>5. **Alpha-glucosidase Inhibitors** slow carbohydrate absorption in the intestines, helping to control post-meal blood sugar levels. Each class offers unique mechanisms and benefits for managing type 2 diabetes.</p></p>
