<p><strong>The Perovskite Solar Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Structure</li><li>Inverted Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The perovskite solar cell market is primarily divided into two types: normal structure and inverted structure. Normal structure cells feature a configuration where the perovskite layer is sandwiched between a transparent conducting oxide and a metal electrode, optimizing light absorption and charge collection. In contrast, inverted structure cells have the perovskite layer situated between an electron transport layer and a hole transport layer, enhancing stability and tunability. Both structures offer distinct advantages in efficiency and fabrication, contributing to their growing market presence.</p></p>
