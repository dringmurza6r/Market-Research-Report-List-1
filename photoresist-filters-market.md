<p><p>The photoresist filters market is primarily divided into capsule filters and cartridge filters. Capsule filters are compact, single-piece units designed for high flow rates, providing efficient filtration in smaller systems. Meanwhile, cartridge filters consist of replaceable filter cartridges housed in a filter housing, offering versatility and ease of maintenance for larger applications. Both types play crucial roles in purifying photoresist solutions in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring the removal of contaminants to achieve high-quality photolithography processes.</p></p>
