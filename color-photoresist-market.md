<p><ul><li>Monochrome Photoresist</li><li>Multicolor Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color photoresist market can be categorized into monochrome and multicolor photoresist types. Monochrome photoresists are designed to respond to a single exposure wavelength, typically used for standard photolithography applications in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, multicolor photoresists can interact with multiple wavelengths, allowing for more intricate patterning and greater complexity in microfabrication processes. This versatility enhances device performance in areas like microelectronics and photonics, driving innovation and demand across various industries.</p></p>
