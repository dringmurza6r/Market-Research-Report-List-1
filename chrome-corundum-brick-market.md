<p><ul><li>Fused Chrome Corundum Brick</li><li>Sintered Chrome Corundum Brick</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chrome Corundum Brick market consists of two primary types: Fused Chrome Corundum Brick and Sintered Chrome Corundum Brick. Fused Chrome Corundum Bricks are produced through melting and cooling alumina and chrome oxides, resulting in high-density, durable bricks ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, Sintered Chrome Corundum Bricks are made through a heating process of raw materials without melting, providing a more cost-effective option with good thermal stability and resistance. Both types are widely used in refractory applications, particularly in metallurgical industries.</p></p>
